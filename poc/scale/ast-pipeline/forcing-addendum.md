## 6. MANDATORY decomposition discipline (forcing-retry variant)

This is a retry pass: an earlier single-shot attempt at this concept was
self-flagged AST-lossy. Experience shows that many such flags are premature —
the attempt stopped at the first hard word instead of decomposing it. Before
you write the record, work through this discipline (privately; your reply is
still ONLY the JSON record):

1. **Enumerate the differentia.** List, to yourself, the 2–5 criterial
   components that distinguish this sense from its genus. Criterial means:
   removing it would let a sibling concept satisfy the definition.
2. **Attempt a primes-only rendering of EACH component** before judging any of
   them unrenderable. Use the full device inventory deliberately:
   - polarity across a time boundary (`BEFORE`/`AFTER` + `NOT` — coming to be,
     ceasing, loss, gain);
   - `CAN` / `NOT`+`CAN` (ability, possibility, blocked action);
   - `WANT` / `DON'T-WANT` / `FEEL` GOOD|BAD (motive, benefit, harm, attitude);
   - `BECAUSE` chains (causation, purpose via WANT+BECAUSE);
   - `IF` (conditionality, threat, contingency);
   - `THINK`/`KNOW`/`SAY`/`WORDS`/`TRUE` (belief, knowledge, assertion,
     convention, undertaking);
   - `LIKE~AS~WAY` (manner and analogy when identity of kind is unavailable);
   - `kindFrame`/`partFrame` + `restrictedBy` (functional description of a
     thing by what people do with it, where it is, what it is part of);
   - spatial manner fillers (`INSIDE`, `ABOVE`, `BELOW`, `NEAR`, `FAR`,
     `TOUCH`, `SIDE`) for configuration and contact;
   - quantity/degree (`MUCH~MANY`, `LITTLE~FEW`, `MORE`, `VERY`, `ONE`, `TWO`,
     `ALL`, `SOME`).
3. **Render function, not fabric.** When the WordNet gloss names an artefact,
   institution, substance, or cultural practice, ask what CRITERIAL work that
   word does in THIS sense, and render that work (what people do with it, want
   from it, what happens because of it). Encyclopedic identity of the artefact
   or institution is usually NOT criterial; do not flag lossy over
   non-criterial encyclopedic residue.
4. **Use the budget.** The cap is 32 clauses; good explications here use 2–5
   top-level clauses, and you may use more when the differentia genuinely
   needs them. Length is not the limiting factor — the only limit is the reach
   of the 65 primes.
5. **Only then self-flag.** Flag `lossy` ONLY if, after genuine attempts, a
   NAMED criterial component still resists rendering — and say in `notes`
   which component and what the AST carries instead. This discipline is not a
   licence for optimism: if criterial meaning truly dropped, flagging
   `faithful` is a worse failure than flagging `lossy`. Judge severely in both
   directions; a merely-plausible generic skeleton is still lossy.
