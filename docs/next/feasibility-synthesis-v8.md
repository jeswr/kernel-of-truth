## 3. The crux: "kernel-specific value is absent" vs "no instrument has yet measured it"

**[STIPULATED reading discipline; weights EXTRAPOLATION]**

The two hypotheses on the table:

- **H-deflate:** the kernel's formal structure adds no measurable value beyond the content it happens to carry, at any seam the programme can afford to test. The typed ledger is the population of an effect that is genuinely ≈0.
- **H-uninstrumented:** the effect is unmeasured because (a) the Tier-2 results were structurally incapable of detecting it, (b) the channels that could detect it (R4; nsk1) are respectively starved (C=8 clusters, 1–2 prompts/concept) and exploratory-then-substrate-compromised, and (c) every purpose-built structure-sensitive instrument has died at a gate before asking.

What would *discriminate* them — the operational content of the crux, now **four conditions** (the fourth added per co-read finding 1, which observed that knull-v2 satisfies the first three for its content-attribution estimand and produced an informative equivalence, making a three-part criterion literally contestable):

> (i) a **validated instrument** (all gates green); (ii) **powered** at a pre-registered margin; (iii) **kernel-vs-matched-generic by construction** (not kernel-vs-nothing); and (iv) **a non-degenerate, structure-sensitive contrast** — the intervention must retain a contrast capable of exercising the kernel's formal apparatus, not one the pipeline collapses to interchangeable content (Tier 2) or a pure content seam (Tier 1).

**No result satisfying all four exists in either direction.** The record therefore cannot yet license H-deflate; but it can, and does, move weight toward it, because H-deflate has acquired a *specific, repeating signature* (ceiling/oracle structure exists; kernel-conditioning captures a sliver as a point estimate; generic content matches kernel content wherever both are aligned), while H-uninstrumented's affirmative support has *weakened* (its one positive lost byte-faithful confirmability; the #39=(a) mitigation preserves a confirmatory path, substrate-relabelled).

**Explanatory-mass allocation** (unchanged from v7 in value; rationale re-grounded on the typed ledger; subjective, binding on nothing) **[EXTRAPOLATION]**: roughly **25% scale/sample artifact / 40% design-space (testable with better instruments and inventory) / 35% thesis genuinely deflationary** (v6: 45/40/15). The hard-deflationary mass rests principally on knull-v2 (valid-instrument equivalence), the CASC-0′ scoped TOST, and R4's sub-margin point estimate — *not* on a six-count, which the co-read correctly disallowed. It stays a minority because the four-condition criterion has never once been met: "hard to demonstrate" still cannot be cleanly separated from "never yet validly attempted at power".
