# Prior Art, Line 1: Deterministic / Canonical / Structure-Preserving Vector Representations of Symbolic & Ontological Knowledge

**Kernel of Truth programme — literature review, line 1**
**Question owned:** where does the kernel's design (Sylvester-Hadamard fixed orthonormal codebook + exact XOR/TPR binding within clauses + unitary HRR composition across clauses + content-hash identity over the canonical AST, all training-free) sit against every prior line that tried to give symbolic/ontological knowledge a vector form? What is genuinely novel, what is re-derivation, which systems came closest, and — the maintainer's explicit lens — **was each line's ceiling a model-capability limit (would a Fable-class LLM authoring/translating explications change the verdict?) or a fundamental data/formalism/evaluation limit?**
**Date:** 2026-07-08. Companion report: `reports/deterministic-concept-vectors.md` (2026-07-07) covers the VSA/TPR capacity mathematics in depth; this report does not repeat those theorems, it cites them, and it owns the KG-embedding / region-embedding / DL-embedding / conceptual-spaces / retrofitting territory plus the positioning and capability-vs-fundamental verdicts.

**Verdict in one paragraph.** Six prior lines tried to put symbolic knowledge into vectors. Four of them (KG embeddings, order/box/hyperbolic embeddings, DL embeddings, retrofitting) are **trained** representations whose vectors are optimisation artifacts — non-canonical by construction, and for the KGE family provably unable to carry rule-level semantics in their standard geometries (Gutiérrez-Basulto & Schockaert, KR 2018). One line (conceptual spaces) is a semantics without a constructor. Exactly one line — VSA/TPR/HDC — has the right algebra (deterministic, compositional, capacity-quantified, decodable-with-cleanup), and it is the line the kernel deliberately re-derives. The critical finding under the maintainer's lens: **the VSA line's ceiling was predominantly a model-capability/ecosystem limit** — it had no scalable author for symbolic structures (hand-built microworlds only), no consumer to inject vectors into, and an evaluation culture that never demanded guarantees — and all three of those constraints are the ones 2026 LLMs remove. The other lines' ceilings are predominantly **fundamental to their formulations** (trained identity cannot be content-addressed; region geometries cannot represent negation; similarity-first objectives cannot fix reference), so better LLMs improve their inputs without changing their verdicts. The kernel's genuine novelty is narrow and specific: the NSM-prime-pinned codebook, and the pairing of content-hash identity with the vector as *two deterministic projections of one canonical AST*. Everything else is deliberate, correctly-attributed re-derivation — which is the strongest position a new system can be in.

## 0. The design under comparison (fixed reference point)

For calibration, the kernel's commitments (from `encoder/src/codebook.ts`, `encoder/src/encoder.ts`, `docs/design-hash-input.md`, `docs/architecture.md`):

- **K1 — Canonical identity:** `urn:kot:` = multibase(sha256) over RFC-8785 JCS bytes of the explication identity payload, profile-header-in-digest. Identity is a pure function of the definition.
- **K2 — Training-free determinism:** no RNG, no seeds, no data; all structure from SHA-256 over fixed labels; byte-identical Float64 output across platforms (X0 goldens).
- **K3 — Exact atomic layer:** ~200 closed atoms (65 NSM primes by chart index, roles, operators, frames, referent indices) = rows of the D×D Sylvester-Hadamard matrix, exactly orthonormal; slot-filler binding = Hadamard/XOR product with **disjoint bit fields** (slotId«8 | fillerId), so every bound pair is a unique Hadamard row — injectivity of pairs is exact, needs D ≥ 8192.
- **K4 — Compositional depth layer:** unitary circular convolution (Plate 1995 §VIII-C) + deterministic permutations + pinned-order superposition; concept references bind the referenced concept's **own canonical vector** (memoised DAG, cycle-fail-closed).
- **K5 — Honest similarity contract:** cosine is banned downstream; the vector's inner product measures *structural overlap*, not meaning (one deep NOT barely moves the vector).
- **K6 — Decodability given the kernel:** decoding = recursive unbind + cleanup against the kernel lexicon itself.

Every verdict below is "relative to K1–K6", not "is this good research".

## 1. Knowledge-graph embeddings: TransE, DistMult, ComplEx, RotatE, BoxE (and HolE)

**What they are.** Learned entity/relation vectors scored for triple plausibility: TransE (Bordes et al., NeurIPS 2013; h + r ≈ t), DistMult (Yang et al., ICLR 2015; bilinear diagonal), ComplEx (Trouillon et al., ICML 2016; complex bilinear), RotatE (Sun et al., ICLR 2019; relations as complex rotations), HolE (Nickel, Rosasco & Poggio, AAAI 2016; scoring by Plate's circular correlation — later shown equivalent to ComplEx, Hayashi & Shimbo 2017), BoxE (Abboud, Ceylan, Lukasiewicz & Salvatori, NeurIPS 2020; entities as points, relations as hyper-rectangles).

**What the theory says.** The family has a real expressiveness literature: TransE cannot model symmetric or 1-N relations; DistMult forces all relations symmetric; ComplEx is fully expressive over facts; RotatE captures symmetry/antisymmetry/inversion/composition patterns; BoxE is **fully expressive with dimensionality linear in |E|** and can **provably inject** a rich class of logical rules into its box configuration (arXiv:2007.06267) — the high-water mark of "geometry carries logic" in this family. The decisive negative result is **Gutiérrez-Basulto & Schockaert, KR 2018 (arXiv:1805.10461)**: viewing relations as regions, the geometries underlying standard KGE scoring functions are *incompatible* with faithfully representing arbitrary rule bases; only **quasi-chained existential rules** can be exactly captured with convex regions. Bourgaux et al. (KR 2024, "Knowledge Base Embeddings: Semantics and Theoretical Properties") continue this program and confirm the gap between what the models score and what a model-theoretic semantics would require.

**Why their ceiling is where it is (lens).** **Fundamental to the formulation, on three independent grounds.** (1) *Identity:* the vectors are functions of (data, seed, hyperparameters, negative-sampling schedule); retraining moves every vector; there is nothing to content-address. K1/K2 are not merely unmet — they are unmeetable inside the paradigm. (2) *Semantics:* the expressiveness theorems above show the geometry cannot carry general rule semantics even in the limit of perfect optimisation. (3) *Objective:* the target is inductive plausibility over incomplete graphs, i.e. deliberately *statistical* generalisation — the opposite of fixation of meaning. A Fable-class LLM changes the *inputs* (it can author better triples, and LLMs already outperform KGEs on many completion tasks, which is quietly deflating this line's original raison d'être) but not the representational contract. **Verdict:** different problem; near-zero overlap with K1–K6. Their legitimate role in the estate is the *relational-plausibility lane*, which sparq's trained KGE lane already owns. HolE matters only as a precedent that HRR composition operators work at web scale.

## 2. Order, box, and hyperbolic embeddings: geometry as partial order

**Order embeddings** (Vendrov, Kiros, Fidler & Urtasun, ICLR 2016): entailment = coordinatewise ≤ in R^n₊, trained with margin losses. The underlying exact question is classical order theory: a poset embeds exactly into the product order on R^k iff its **Dushnik–Miller order dimension** ≤ k (1941), and deciding order dimension ≥ 3 is **NP-complete** (Yannakakis 1982). So the *deterministic exact* version of this line hits a complexity wall in general; the learned version is the relaxation that gave up exactness.

**Box embeddings** (Vilnis, Li, Murty & McCallum, ACL 2018 — box-lattice measures; Dasgupta et al., NeurIPS 2020 — Gumbel boxes): concepts as axis-aligned hyper-rectangles; boxes are closed under intersection, so conjunction is geometric and containment is entailment, with a probabilistic (volume) semantics. Two facts matter for us. First, the Gumbel-box fix exists because hard boxes have **zero-gradient plateaus** when disjoint — the geometry actively resists gradient optimisation; a *constructive* approach would not care. Second, the complement of a box is not a box: **negation is unrepresentable in the region calculus** — a formalism limit, since NSM explications contain NOT at arbitrary depth.

**Hyperbolic embeddings** (Nickel & Kiela, NeurIPS 2017 Poincaré; ICML 2018 Lorentz; Ganea, Bécigneul & Hofmann, ICML 2018 entailment cones): hierarchy in few dimensions, entailment as cone membership. The star for our purposes is **Sarkar (2011) as scaled by Sala, De Sa, Gu & Ré, "Representation Tradeoffs for Hyperbolic Embeddings" (ICML 2018, arXiv:1804.03329)**: a **combinatorial, optimisation-free construction** that embeds any tree in 2-dimensional hyperbolic space with arbitrarily low distortion — on WordNet it achieves MAP 0.989 in 2 dimensions where Nickel-Kiela's trained construction got 0.87 in 200. This is the most successful *explicitly training-free canonical embedding* in the published literature, and it comes with the honest price tag: the paper proves a **precision-dimension tradeoff** — the bits per coordinate needed grow with tree depth and degree, so the determinism is bought with unbounded numerical precision (the same finite-precision moral as the companion report's §0).

**Why their ceiling is where it is (lens).** **Fundamental — formalism scope.** These geometries encode exactly one thing: a partial order / tree metric. No roles, no predicates, no coreference, no negation, no n-ary structure — an NSM explication is not a poset element. The trained variants additionally forfeit K1/K2. The Sala construction did not "stop" from lack of model capability; it delivered everything its formalism can express, and the formalism saturates at hierarchy. An LLM authoring richer structure gives this line *nothing to embed it in*. **Verdict:** strong prior art for one sub-problem — a deterministic taxonomy lane (sparq's taxonomy ancestor-bag vectoriser is the same idea in VSA clothing) — and a proof-of-concept that training-free + provable is a publishable, winning combination. The kernel should cite Sala et al. as the precedent that constructive beats learned *when the structure class is fully understood*, which is exactly the kernel's bet for capped explications.

## 3. Description-logic embeddings: ELEm, BoxEL, Box2EL, TransBox, OWL2Vec*

**The line.** Embed EL++ ontologies so that geometry models the logic: **ELEm** (Kulmanov, Liu-Wei, Yan & Hoehndorf, IJCAI 2019 — "EL Embeddings: geometric construction of models for EL++", concepts as n-balls); **ELBE** (Peng et al. 2022) and **BoxEL** (Xiong, Potyka, Tran, Nayyeri & Staab, ISWC 2022) move to boxes because balls are not closed under intersection; **Box2EL** (Jackermeier, Chen & Horrocks, WWW 2024, arXiv:2301.11118) adds **role boxes + a bumping mechanism** with a **soundness proof** (zero loss ⇒ the geometry is a model of the ontology) and fixes one-to-many/role-inclusion failures; **TransBox** (Yang, Chen & Sattler, WWW 2025, arXiv:2410.14571) constructs **EL++-closed** embeddings — the geometric operations compose so that *any* EL++ concept expression has a well-defined region, SOTA on complex-axiom prediction. **OWL2Vec\*** (Chen, Hu, Jiménez-Ruiz, Horrocks et al., *Machine Learning* 110, 2021) is the corpus lane: random walks + literals → word2vec — purely statistical, no guarantees, strong on lexical signal.

**Why this is the closest *formal* cousin of the kernel.** This is the only prior line that (a) starts from *definitions* in a closed logical grammar, (b) proves **faithfulness theorems** relating geometry to semantics, and (c) targets concept-level (not entity-level) representation. The structural analogy to the kernel is real: closed grammar → typed structure → geometric object.

**Why their ceiling is where it is (lens).** **Split verdict — and this is the most instructive case in the survey.**
- *Fundamental-by-choice:* every one of these systems **optimises** toward a model instead of **constructing** one, because their evaluation target is *inductive* (predict plausible missing subsumptions, protein–protein interactions). Soundness theorems are conditional ("if the loss reaches zero…"), SGD never reaches zero, and the reached minimum is seed-dependent — K1/K2 unmeetable as formulated. Yet EL has the **canonical-model property** — least models computable in polynomial time — so a *deterministic geometric canonical-model construction* was always mathematically available. Nobody built it, because a canonical model only answers deduction, and deduction already had reasoners (ELK). The line's non-canonicity is thus not an accident but a consequence of chasing what learning is for.
- *Fundamental-formalism:* EL++ deliberately lacks negation and disjunction; box/ball regions cannot represent complements anyway (§2). NSM explications need NOT, SOMETIMES-like quantification, and speech-act framing — outside the geometry's reach. Region semantics cannot be the kernel's whole answer *in principle*, only its taxonomy/conjunction fragment.
- *Model-capability-limited (the interesting part):* the reason deduction-faithful embeddings had thin value is that real ontologies are **definition-poor** — mostly bare subsumptions, few full definitions (GO, SNOMED partially). The knowledge-authoring bottleneck capped what there was to be faithful *to*. A Fable-class LLM that authors full definitional structure at scale (which is precisely what the kernel's explication pipeline is) changes the economics: with rich canonical definitions, the constructive/canonical route stops being a curiosity and becomes the natural representation, while the inductive route loses its monopoly. **The DL-embedding line stopped one step short of the kernel because, pre-LLM, there was nothing at scale worth constructing canonically.**

**Verdict:** closest formal prior art; cite Box2EL/TransBox for the faithfulness-theorem *style* the kernel's own guarantees should be written in; OWL2Vec* is a mandatory *statistical baseline* for any downstream evaluation. An open middle path worth an experiment: a deterministic canonical-model box construction for the negation-free fragment of the kernel (geometric subsumption queries over the taxonomy layer) — regions for the fragment where regions are honest, VSA for the rest.

## 4. Gärdenfors conceptual spaces

**What it is.** Concepts = convex regions in spaces of quality dimensions; prototypes + Voronoi tessellations; convexity explains generalisation (Gärdenfors, *Conceptual Spaces*, MIT Press 2000). Computational instantiations are hand-modelled (Bechberger & Kühnberger, arXiv:1706.06366/1707.05165 — fuzzy star-shaped sets with computable operations); a recent bridge combines VSA operations with conceptual-space semantics (arXiv:2411.08684).

**Why its ceiling is where it is (lens).** **Split, leaning model-capability for instantiation, fundamental for structure.** The theory's famous gap is that it provides **no constructive map from a symbolic definition to a region** — the quality dimensions and coordinates were always hand-authored, which is exactly the kind of bottleneck LLMs now remove (Schockaert's group and others have begun eliciting conceptual-space dimensions and entity placements from LLMs). So the *instantiation* ceiling was a model-capability limit, and it is lifting as predicted by the lens. But conceptual spaces have **no binding algebra**: no mechanism for roles, clauses, coreference, or recursive definition — the theory describes similarity between concepts, not the composition of definitions. That gap is architectural, and it is the gap VSA fills. **Verdict:** conceptual spaces are the right *semantics for the domain/world layer* (graded quality dimensions — sparq's thermometer/QUDT/enum vectorisers are a working conceptual-spaces lane in all but name), and a warning: the kernel's structural-overlap similarity (K5) is *not* conceptual-space similarity, and the programme should never claim it is. Meaning-level similarity, if wanted, must be computed relationally over explication content, not read off the vector geometry.

## 5. Holographic / VSA / TPR / hyperdimensional computing (the kernel's own ancestry)

Covered quantitatively in the companion report (§§1–2, 7); here only the positioning facts and the lens.

**The line.** Smolensky's TPR (AIJ 46, 1990: exact role-filler binding via tensor products, exact unbinding with independent roles, exponential cost in depth); Plate's HRR (IEEE TNN 6(3), 1995: circular-convolution compression of TPR, unitary vectors kill the depth-variance penalty, cleanup memories for decoding); Kanerva's HDC (Cogn. Comput. 2009; and the 2010 "What's the Dollar of Mexico?" paper — **training-free analogical inference by XOR binding**); Gayler's MAP and the Jackendoff-challenges argument (arXiv:cs/0412059); Eliasmith's **Semantic Pointer Architecture** — semantic pointers are compressed recursive structure vectors with cleanup, driving Spaun's 2.5M-neuron cognitive model (Eliasmith et al., *Science* 338, 2012; *How to Build a Brain*, OUP 2013); **resonator networks** for factorising bound products with operational capacity ∝ D² (Frady/Kent/Olshausen/Sommer, *Neural Computation* 32(12), 2020); capacity theory with constants (Plate 1995 App. B; Frady, Kleyko & Sommer, *Neural Computation* 30(6), 2018: 0.2–0.5 bits/dim recoverable; Thomas, Dasgupta & Rosing, JAIR 72, 2021: d = O(s ln m), inner product provably estimates feature-multiset overlap); **GrapHD** (Poduval et al., *Frontiers in Neuroscience* 16, 2022: whole graph → one hypervector → edge-set reconstruction, ~40 dims/edge). One precedent to log precisely: **Hadamard/Walsh orthogonal codebooks already exist in the HDC hardware literature** (e.g. "On-the-Fly Hadamard Hypervector Processing for Efficient HDC", 2025; orthogonal item memories from Hadamard/Walsh/Gold sequences for accuracy and O(1) generation) — as *generic uncorrelated item memories* for classification pipelines. The kernel's Hadamard basis is therefore not novel *as a codebook*; what is novel is what it is pinned to (§7).

**Why did this line — with the right algebra since 1995 — never produce a canonical concept kernel? (lens).** **Predominantly a model-capability/ecosystem limit, in three concrete mechanisms:**
1. **No author.** Every VSA/SPA demonstration encoded hand-built microstructures: Plate's toy frames and analogies, SPA's working-memory tasks, HDC's sensor features. Producing tens of thousands of *canonical symbolic definitions* to encode required either armies of trained semanticists (NSM's actual history: a few hundred hand-crafted explications in 40 years) or a machine that reads and writes natural language reliably. That machine did not exist. It now does: Baartmans et al., "Towards Universal Semantics with Large Language Models" (arXiv:2505.11764, 2025) show even fine-tuned 1B/8B models draft NSM explications that beat GPT-4o on their legality/substitutability/cross-translatability metrics — and a Fable-class model inside the kernel's validator loop is far beyond that baseline. **This is the single strongest datapoint for the maintainer's lens: the binding constraint of the best-suited prior line was authoring capability, and it measurably lifted in 2025.**
2. **No consumer.** Before LLMs there was no host system into which a concept vector could be *injected* to do anything except more VSA. HDC therefore drifted to where its vectors had customers: low-power classification hardware (ISOLET, EMG, edge accelerators) — tasks where random codebooks suffice and none of K1/K5/K6 matter, which is why the field never built them.
3. **Evaluation culture.** Benchmarks rewarded accuracy, not guarantees; a system whose selling points are determinism, content-addressability, and provable capacity had no leaderboard to win. (The fundamental residuals — capacity ceiling ~0.5 bits/dim, similarity = structural overlap, cleanup-memory dependence — are real but *quantified*, and the kernel's caps are engineered inside them; companion report §7.)

**Verdict:** the kernel is this line's continuation, and should say so plainly. The re-derivations are correct and correctly attributed in the code comments. The lens answer — VSA stalled on authoring/consumer/evaluation, all capability-or-ecosystem constraints that 2026 removes — is the load-bearing argument for why *now* is the right time for this programme, and it should appear in the prospectus in exactly this form.

## 6. Retrofitting and ConceptNet Numberbatch

**What they are.** **Faruqui et al., "Retrofitting Word Vectors to Semantic Lexicons" (NAACL 2015):** post-hoc adjustment of trained word vectors toward graph neighbours by minimising a **convex** objective — unique global optimum, closed-form Jacobi iteration, deterministic *given* (base vectors, graph). **ConceptNet Numberbatch** (Speer, Chin & Havasi, AAAI 2017): retrofitting-at-scale over ConceptNet + word2vec/GloVe/fastText; for years the SOTA on word-similarity benchmarks; distributed as a **versioned, public artifact keyed by ConceptNet URIs**.

**Why their ceiling is where it is (lens).** **Fundamental — formalism.** The convexity is a nice canonical-ish touch (unique optimum), but the inputs are distributional: the vectors inherit corpus statistics, conflate word senses, drift with every release, and have no compositional structure, no decodability, and no identity function from a definition. There is no place in the formalism where a definition could *enter*; an LLM improves the base embeddings and the graph, and the output remains a statistical summary of usage. **Verdict:** near-zero technical overlap, but **the closest *social/artifact* precedent in the entire survey**: Numberbatch proved there is demand for a *published, versioned, URI-keyed concept-vector artifact* consumed by systems that did not train it. The kernel should copy its distribution discipline (versioned releases, stable keys, changelogs) while replacing its epistemology (statistical → canonical). It is also the mandatory "cheap statistical baseline" for any claim that structure-derived vectors buy something.

## 7. Explicitly training-free attempts, collected

The complete set of published training-free vector constructions found, each with its stopping point:

| System | What it deterministically encodes | Guarantees | Why it stopped |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sala/De Sa/Gu/Ré combinatorial hyperbolic (ICML 2018) + Sarkar 2011 | trees/hierarchies | arbitrarily low distortion; proven precision-dimension tradeoff | formalism saturates at partial order; precision grows with depth×degree |
| FastRP (Chen et al., CIKM 2019) | graph nodes via iterated random projection | JL-style, seed-fixed | node features only; no structure semantics, no decode |
| Random sentence encoders (Wieting & Kiela, ICLR 2019) | word sequences | none (empirical parity with trained) | showed trained encoders add little — a null result, not a system |
| Tree Echo State Networks (Gallicchio & Micheli, 2013) | trees | contractive determinism | provable root-bias: deep structure exponentially attenuated |
| GraphHD (Nunes et al., DATE 2022) | graphs → hypervectors | deterministic given codebook | classification target only |
| GrapHD (Poduval et al., 2022) | graphs → hypervectors, **with reconstruction** | edge recovery at ~40 dims/edge | demo-scale; no identity layer, no semantics |
| Kanerva 2010 "Dollar of Mexico" | analogical mappings via XOR | exact unbinding | never scaled beyond vignettes — no author for content |
| Cryptographic content addressing (Unison language's content-addressed ASTs; IPFS multihash; RDF canonicalization RDFC-1.0) | identity of structures | collision-resistant injectivity | identity only — no geometry, no similarity, by design |

The pattern is uniform: each attempt delivers a subset of {determinism, similarity, decodability, identity, semantics} and stops where its *application* stopped demanding more. No prior system assembled **identity + geometry + composition over a semantic basis** into one artifact — and the searched-for combination "NSM primes + vector symbolic architecture / hypervector" returns **nothing** (verified 2026-07-08; the NSM computational literature goes to LLM explication generation, not vectorisation).

## 8. Positioning: novel / re-derived / closest

**Genuinely novel (to the limit of this search, which should be re-run before any external claim):**
1. **NSM primes as the pinned atomic codebook of a compositional vector encoder.** No prior hit combines the NSM closed semantic basis with any vector-symbolic construction. The 65-prime chart-index pinning (codebook rows 1–65) makes the *semantic theory itself* part of the encoder's versioned contract.
2. **Identity ⊗ geometry pairing:** the URN (JCS+SHA-256) and the vector (Hadamard/HRR) are two deterministic projections of the *same canonical AST*, so the vector is effectively content-addressed and byte-reproducible from the definition alone. Prior art has each half (Unison/IPFS for identity; VSA for geometry); nothing pairs them.
3. **Bit-field XOR-injective slot-filler layout** (slotId«8|fillerId over Sylvester-Hadamard rows, exact zero crosstalk, D≥8192 fail-closed): an engineering novelty — HDC hardware uses Hadamard item memories for *efficiency*, not as a semantically pinned lexicon with exact pair injectivity.
4. **Kernel-as-cleanup-memory doubling as the published artifact:** the decode dependency (K6) is turned into the product (a Numberbatch-style versioned lexicon).

**Deliberate re-derivation (attributed, correctly, in the code):** exact TPR within a clause (Smolensky 1990); unitary circular convolution across depth (Plate 1995 §VIII-C); permutations for position (Kanerva); reference-not-inline = Plate's chunking / Eliasmith's semantic pointers, upgraded from per-model random vectors to a global canonical content-addressed lexicon; all capacity arithmetic (Frady 2018, Thomas 2021).

**Closest prior systems and why each stopped:**
- **SPA/Spaun (Eliasmith):** closest *architecture* — recursive structured vectors + cleanup at system scale. Stopped at cognitive modelling: random codebooks, no canonical identity, hand-authored content (authoring = capability limit; identity = never a goal).
- **Box2EL / TransBox:** closest *formal program* — closed grammar, faithfulness theorems, concept-level. Stopped at trained inductive prediction: canonical construction had no payoff on definition-poor ontologies (capability limit on the data side, purpose choice on the method side), and region geometry cannot host negation (fundamental).
- **ConceptNet Numberbatch:** closest *artifact* — public versioned URI-keyed concept vectors. Stopped at lexical statistics: no formalism slot for definitions (fundamental).
- **GrapHD:** closest *encoder* — deterministic structure→vector with demonstrated reconstruction. Stopped at benchmark demos: no identity, no semantic basis, no consumer (ecosystem limit).
- **Sala et al. combinatorial hyperbolic:** closest *methodological precedent* — training-free with proofs, decisively beating trained baselines. Stopped at trees (fundamental formalism scope).

## 9. The capability-vs-fundamental scorecard

| Line | Ceiling | Mechanism | Does a Fable-class explication author change the verdict? |
|---|---|---|---|
| KG embeddings | **Fundamental** | trained identity non-addressable; geometry provably can't hold rule bases (KR 2018); objective is statistical | No — better triples in, statistical summary out |
| Order/box/hyperbolic | **Fundamental** (formalism scope) | encodes partial order only; boxes can't negate; exact order-embedding NP-hard; hyperbolic precision grows with depth | No — nothing in the formalism to receive richer structure |
| DL embeddings (EL++ family) | **Split** | trained-inductive by purpose (fundamental as formulated); but the *reason* canonical construction had no payoff was definition-poor ontologies | **Yes, partially** — LLM-authored full definitions make the constructive/canonical route the natural one; negation gap remains fundamental |
| Conceptual spaces | **Split** | no constructive symbolic→region map; dimensions were hand-authored (capability, now lifting); no binding algebra (fundamental) | **Yes for instantiation**, no for structure |
| VSA/TPR/HDC | **Model-capability/ecosystem** | no author (hand-built microworlds), no consumer, no guarantee-valuing benchmark; fundamental residuals (capacity, overlap-similarity, cleanup) are quantified and engineered-around | **Yes — this is the kernel's core bet, and DeepNSM (arXiv:2505.11764) is the first external evidence the authoring constraint lifted** |
| Retrofitting/Numberbatch | **Fundamental** | no formalism slot for definitions; distributional inheritance; release drift | No — better bases, same epistemology |

## 10. Implications for the decisive experiments

1. **Baselines are dictated by this survey:** any downstream-utility experiment must include (a) Numberbatch (statistical concept vectors), (b) OWL2Vec* or a KGE over the same concept graph (statistical relational), and (c) a random-codebook VSA ablation (does *canonical/semantic pinning* buy anything over any fixed random codebook? — the Wieting-Kiela null looms here and must be confronted head-on).
2. **Test the hinge assumption directly:** the programme's "why now" is that authoring was VSA's ceiling. Measure Fable-class explication quality inside the validator loop using Baartmans et al.'s legality/substitutability/cross-translatability metrics (reusable as-is) against their DeepNSM/GPT-4o numbers. If authoring quality is not decisively better than DeepNSM-8B, the why-now argument weakens materially.
3. **Never let similarity claims drift:** K5's cosine ban is corroborated by every line surveyed (region embeddings *make* geometry mean entailment and pay with trained non-canonicity; the kernel refuses that trade). Any experiment reporting vector similarity must label it "structural overlap" and include the NOT-flip pathology (X3) as a standing negative control.
4. **Consider the open middle path:** a deterministic canonical-model *box* construction for the negation-free taxonomy fragment of the kernel (EL-style subsumption as geometric containment, constructed not learned) — cheap, novel relative to §3, and would give the kernel a geometric-entailment lane where geometry is honest.
5. **Decodability claims must be phrased "given the kernel"** (K6); benchmark multi-factor decode against resonator networks (capacity ∝ D²) rather than reinventing.
6. **Copy Numberbatch's distribution discipline** (versioned releases, stable content-addressed keys, changelogs) — the artifact precedent proves the consumption model; the kernel's identity layer makes it verifiable for the first time.
7. **Re-run the novelty search before any external pitch** (prospectus rule already in architecture.md §0.1): the claims in §8 are "to the limit of searches on 2026-07-08".
